Bitsy Room Designs

A pixel-art game called 'Reverie for Two' made with Bitsy for ENG263 Play and Games course. The player controls the Curator in a museum to interact with NPCs and collect artefacts to complete an exhibit that holds the truth about the Curator's tragic past. Each room is designed with a colour palette to reflect an emotion and conflict.
